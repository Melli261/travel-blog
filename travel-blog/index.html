Hi, I'm Melli

I'm 20 years old and currently pursuing my degree in Media Computer Science at IU. My goal is to become a software developer while also pursuing my passion for videography as a freelancer.
One of my biggest passions, aside from technology and videography, is traveling. I absolutely love discovering new places and immersing myself in different cultures. I’m particularly drawn to the ocean—probably because I’m a certified diver. There's something incredibly peaceful and freeing about being underwater, exploring a whole new world filled with marine life and untouched beauty.
My most recent adventure was a month-long journey through Asia, which I balanced alongside my studies. Traveling alone for the first time, especially so far from home, was both exhilarating and intimidating. From the bustling streets of Hanoi to the tropical serenity of Bali and the golden beaches of Phuket, this trip allowed me to grow in ways I hadn’t expected.
Originally, this blog started as a project for my studies, but I plan to keep it alive and expand it with stories from future travels.
